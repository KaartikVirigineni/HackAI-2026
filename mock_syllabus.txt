Cybersecurity 101: Foundations of Cyber Defense
Instructor: Agent X
Course Overview: This course covers the fundamental principles of cybersecurity, including threat modeling, basic cryptography, network defense strategies, and incident response.
Key Topics for Midterm Exam:
1. The CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability)
2. Difference between Symmetric and Asymmetric Encryption
3. Types of Malware: Ransomware, Spyware, Trojans, Worms
4. Social Engineering: Phishing, Spear Phishing, Whaling
5. Basic Network Defense: Firewalls (Packet filtering vs Stateful), Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS)

Students must be able to define these concepts and apply them to hypothetical security breach scenarios.
